About Me

Hi, I’m Ana — the woman behind creativity, designs, marketing, and more. I enjoy designing web pages, brochures, logos, signs, t-shirts, presentations, and other marketing materials. I have an eye for intuitive design, color, and layout with a strong talent for creating custom graphic designs for all social media platforms. I understand the principles of effective branding, marketing communication, and user experience design, and I know the significant role that design can play in achieving your business objectives. I am working towards my degree in web design. I am highly organized, and detail-oriented with experience in Adobe Creative Suite, including AfterEffects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I have demonstrated experience in creating responsive website designs using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that follow user-interface design principles.
